A little about my journey

Growing up in an information technology age, I had always been deeply fascinated with how the internet works- the speed, social interactions, world wide interconnectivity and application development. This fascination led me into choosing a career in Cyber Security. This is for the purpose of protecting the internet users, digital property and information from threats while maintaining order on the internet.

Outside coursework, I keep a running list of small projects — this portfolio being one of them. I'm especially interested in how web technologies can support academic activities itself, which is why the Academic Planner on this site is not just a feature but a tool I use to track my academic progress.
